About Us

At Dojo Code, we are passionate about fostering a community of coding enthusiasts. Our website serves as a hub for coding clubs, events, and resources that help individuals of all skill levels improve their programming abilities. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced coder seeking to connect with like-minded individuals, our platform offers something for everyone. Join us and be a part of a vibrant community that shares your love for coding and continuous learning.

Our History

Dojo Code was founded in 2010 with the mission to create a supportive and engaging environment for coders of all levels. Over the years, we have grown into a thriving community with thousands of members worldwide. Our events and resources have helped countless individuals improve their coding skills and achieve their professional goals.

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in the power of community and strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members.
  • Learning: Continuous learning is at the heart of what we do. We provide resources and opportunities for members to expand their knowledge and skills.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and innovation in all aspects of coding and technology.
  • Support: We offer support and guidance to help our members overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
